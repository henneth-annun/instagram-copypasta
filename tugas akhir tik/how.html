How It Works

The copypastas are generated by ChatGPT to trick the algorithm into categorizing the post as an informative post, therefore boosting its views. The long text also increases engagement by allowing viewers to read to the end of the caption. The first ever recorded use of the copypasta is the Mercedes CLR GTR, started by @oyatillo__.oo8 on January 21st, 2024
